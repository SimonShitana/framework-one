About Me

Helena Nelago Amunyela

Metallurgical Engineering Student (B.Eng Hons)

I am a dedicated Metallurgical Engineering student at the University of Namibia, driven by a deep curiosity for how materials are transformed from raw ores into high-performance alloys and functional components. My passion lies in creating efficient, sustainable extraction methods that minimize environmental impact while maximizing value.

My academic journey focuses on extractive metallurgy, phase transformations, and advanced material characterization. I believe that metallurgists play a crucial role in shaping a circular economy — where waste becomes resource, and innovation serves both industry and planet.

"The future of metals is not just in mining more, but in refining smarter."
